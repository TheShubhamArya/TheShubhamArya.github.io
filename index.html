About Me

As a computer scientist, I aspire to build applications that millions of people around the world can use to make the world a more sustainable, habitable and happier place to live.

In just 22 years on this planet, I have had the pleasure to learn some interesting things, meet extraordinary people, and visit amazing places around the world. This website captures a fraction of my work and experiences which should help you to get to know me better.

I am no prodigy but I am someone who is inspired easily. I look at a thing that fascinates me and I get motivated to do something better than that. I have this innate quality to learn a concept rapidly and then apply that in a practical use. For instance, I was motivated to develop my own apps that people could use globally. In just 2 months, I was able to accomplish this feat when I pushed my first app on the App Store.

While I have had my fair share of success, I don't mind occassional failures. I believe that the learning I get through my failures provides me a far greater understanding of the situation which makes me a better problem solver for the future. Moreover, I have a greater drive to succeed when I encounter difficulties. For example, after I had to decline an intenrship from Apple due to coronavirus in summer of 2021, I was impelled to create my third app for the App Store through which I gained some advanced skills that I may not have otherwise.

This website is the story of my life. Please reach out to me if you like my work and have ideas to share!
