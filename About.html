Hello.

My name is Shubham Arya. I am a junior at the University of Texas at Arlington majoring in computer science. This website is made by me as a way for me to display my talents, interests, hobbies and projects. Developing mobile applications and website are some of my favorite hobbies. Apart from this, I love watching and playing cricket. I am also a big fan of The Office and Brooklyn Nine-Nine, and I can watch them on a loop all day long. My all time favorite food are biryani and butter chicken. Lastly, I like to constantly work on something, and checking things off THE list is my biggest motivation.

I was born in New Delhi, India. I stayed in Gurgaon, Haryana for the first 10 years of my life where I studied at Delhi Public School, Gurgaon. My family then moved to Chennai, Tamil Nadu and we stayed there for another 9 years. In Chennai, I studied at Vaels International School, ECR and The PSBB Millennium School, OMR. In 12th grade, I gave the SATs and got accepted by the University of Texas at Arlington where I am currenty studying.
