Description

Wallet helps you manage your finances. Have you ever felt that you do not have complete control over your budget? Or have you ever felt the need to manage your money to cut down your spending? If so, then Wallet is the perfect app for you.

Wallet: Budget Manager lets you create different categories for your expenses. You can add a transaction under the category it falls under. These categories are then used for analyzing your spending. Each category can have an unlimited transaction that stores a variety of information about it. With our sleek design and intuitive user interface, users can add a transaction into a category quickly and easily. These categories are put under periods that are automatically update every month. You do not need to add the same categories again every month.

Wallet creates beautiful and informative graphs from your data. This type of visual representation helps you in understanding your spending. We provide category-wise statistics that give you graphs of categories for different periods. You can also filter out transactions based on the type of transaction medium. Do you want to know your trends for credit card spending? With a transaction mode filter, you can conveniently get these trends.

We also provide a full report for all the months that helps to understand your spending over some time. You also have access to all the line graphs, bar graphs, and pie charts of all the past months. You can look back and see how you spent your money for each month.

An integral part of Wallet is the currency converter. Get almost real-time conversions for your transactions. No need to go on the internet to convert your transactions and then input them on the app. We do that with just a couple of clicks. You can also change your default currency anytime you want.

Wallet also comes up with a variety of customizations. You can edit your categories by selecting an image from the hundreds of image icons we provide in the app. You can also select colors for a category from literally unlimited colors. You can also add a budget for each category so you do not go over the limit for that category.

Apart from this, Wallet also has Face ID, Touch ID, or password protection so only you can access your information. Wallet adapts to dark mode to ease your eyes. You can also select different app icons to be displayed on your screen.

You can unlock all these functionalities by getting Wallet Plus. With a small In-App purchase, you can use the app to its complete potential. Wallet Plus contains full reports, category-wise statistics, currency converter, and many more things. I am sure a one-time payment will make your experience while using the app worth it.

Wallet: Budget Manager has been designed carefully with great thought put into the app design to make the users have the best experience while using it. If you loved the app, please share it with as many people as you can and write a review. Your feedback is highly appreciated and it will help me add newer features to this app.

Wallet: Budget Manager is available on iPhones with iOS 14 and above.
