About Me

As a computer scientist, I aspire to build applications that millions of people can use to make the world a more sustainable, habitable, and happier place to live.

In 22 years, I have had the pleasure to learn many things, meet extraordinary people, and travel around the world. This website captures a fraction of my work and experiences that should help you to know me better.

I am no prodigy, but I am someone who is inspired easily. I look at things that fascinate me, and I get motivated to do something better than that. I have this innate quality to learn a concept rapidly and then apply that to practical use. For instance, I was motivated to develop apps that people could use globally. Within two months, I accomplished this feat when I pushed my first app on the App Store.

While I have had my fair share of success, I don't mind occasional failures. I believe that failures improve a person that makes them a better problem solver for the future. When I encounter such difficult situations, I have a greater drive to succeed. For example, after I had to decline an internship from Apple due to coronavirus in the summer of 2021, I was impelled to create my third app, through which I gained some advanced skills that I may not have otherwise.

This website is the story of my life. Please reach out to me if you like my work and have ideas to share!
