Description

Money-Wise helps you manage your finances. Have you ever felt that you do not have complete control over your budget? Or have you ever felt the need to manage your money to cut down your spending? If so, then Money-Wise is the perfect app for you.

Add details about a transaction. You can add amount, name, image, location, date, transaction mode, and so much more like:
  • Convert currencies in real-time when adding a transaction
  • Automatically repeat transactions every month
  • Set notifications for a transaction to never forget it
  • Mark a transaction important.
Analyze your spending in different ways like:
  • Category-wise- with detailed graphs and spending analysis, you can find how you spend in a category.
  • Transaction mode- find which transaction modes you use the mode and find informative graphs on it.
  • Time- filter your transactions depending on when you spent your money.
  • Monthly analysis- Get your monthly report with some detailed analysis on where you spent the most and where you can save more.
  • All-time analysis- Find information for every transaction that you have EVER put in the app and get analysis on it.
More features include:
  • iCloud sync across all your Apple devices
  • Real-time currency converter
  • Unlimited customizations for categories
  • Privacy- Your data stays on your device
  • Support for dark mode
  • App customizations: app icon, chart colors, app lock, animations
